Ludo Tournament


Ludo tournament conducted by students of production engineering department Government Engineering College Thrissur. Anybody can participate in this tournament. Try your luck maybe you would be crowned as the ludo king. Yes the king gets treasure.
The game is conducted in LUDO KINGTM.
32 players and one crown.
2 players will be eliminated each game.
Entry fee 10Rs.
First prize 150Rs
Second Prize 100Rs
Third Prize 55Rs
